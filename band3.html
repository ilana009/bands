Frank Zappa

Frank Vincent Zappa (21/12/1940-04/12/1993) is one of the top 20th century composers and guitarists. He died of prostate cancer.

Songs from progressive rock to Jazz to classical

Many famous then-upcoming musicians would join him on tour:

  • Vinnie Colaiuta
  • George Duke

  • Terry Bozzio
